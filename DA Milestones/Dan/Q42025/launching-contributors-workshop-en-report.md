## Objective

The Launching Contributors Workshop is designed to activate and onboard new developers into the Cardano open-source ecosystem. Through mentor-led sessions and hands-on coding challenges, participants learn how to set up their environment, interact with Intersect repositories, and submit their first pull requests (PRs) that create real impact.
