## Task Objective

Host 1 regional or language-specific workshop per quarter

## Workshop Summary

**Event:** Launching Contributors Workshop [FR]  
**Date:** November 20, 2025  
**Language:** French (Français)  
**Format:** Virtual Workshop via Google Meet  
**Meeting Link:** https://meet.google.com/mma-jtnv-tzg  
**Theme:** Practical Contribution & Live Collaboration

## Workshop Agenda

The workshop provided French-speaking developers with hands-on experience in contributing to Intersect open-source projects through the following activities:

1. Recap and clarification (en français)
2. Guided exploration of Intersect repositories
3. Pull request preparation, submission, and review process
4. Live contribution challenge (commit + PR submission)
5. Closing and post-workshop evaluation instructions
