### Tech Experiment/Demo Q2 2026

## Local Cardano Payment Detector Workshop

**Date:** May 19, 2026  
**Location:** DA Satellite Event, Lisbon, Portugal

### Overview

Delivered a hands-on technical workshop at the Developer Advocates Satellite Event in Lisbon, demonstrating how to build a local Cardano payment detector. Participants followed a step-by-step tutorial covering wallet setup, transaction monitoring, and payment detection logic using the Cardano emulator environment.

### Details

- Walked attendees through building a local payment detection system on Cardano
- Covered key concepts: address generation, UTxO monitoring, and transaction parsing
- Participants ran the demo locally during the session, gaining practical experience with Cardano tooling
- The workshop served as both a technical experiment and a developer onboarding opportunity

### Impact

- Showcased a practical use case that newcomers can build and extend
- Provided attendees with a reusable tutorial they can reference independently
- Demonstrated Cardano development tooling in a real workshop setting
