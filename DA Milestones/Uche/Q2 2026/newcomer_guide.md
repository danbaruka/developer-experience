### Overview

Completed a step-by-step newcomer guide for the Cardano Build Emulator, contributed directly to the Cardano Developer Portal. This guide helps new developers get started building on Cardano by walking them through the emulator setup and usage.

### Impact

- Lowers the barrier to entry for newcomers starting projects on Cardano
- Improves existing Developer Portal resources with practical, hands-on content
- Enables developers to experiment in a safe emulator environment before deploying on-chain
