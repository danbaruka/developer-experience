# Developer Onboarding Report – Q2 2026

## Overview

Onboarded 4 developers into the Cardano ecosystem during the Developer Advocates Satellite Event in Lisbon, Portugal on May 19, 2026. Participants were introduced to Cardano development through hands-on technical workshops conducted at the event.

## Onboarding Activities

- Guided participants through setting up local Cardano development environments
- Walked developers through the [Local Cardano Payment Detector](https://devex.intersectmbo.org/docs/tutorials/local-cardano-payment-detector) tutorial
- Introduced Intersect, the DevEx Working Group, and contribution pathways
- Provided mentorship on next steps for building on Cardano

## Impact

- 4 new developers onboarded into the Cardano ecosystem
- Developers gained practical experience with Cardano tooling during the workshop
- Created a pipeline for continued engagement and contributions post-event

## Conclusion

This milestone was completed successfully through direct onboarding at the DA Satellite Event in Lisbon, combining in-person mentorship with hands-on technical workshops to bring new developers into the ecosystem.
